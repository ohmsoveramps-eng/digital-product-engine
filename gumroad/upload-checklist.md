### Booking Confirmation Email Template

Subject: MPC Library Cleanup Audit - next details needed

Thanks for booking the MPC Library Cleanup Audit.

Please reply with:

- MPC model
- MPC OS version, if known
- Standalone, controller, or both
- Drive/folder size
- Drive type: SD card, USB drive, external SSD, internal storage, or other
- Whether this is your authoritative MPC drive or a copied/test drive
- Whether a full independent backup exists
- Main issue: duplicate samples, noisy Browser/Quick Search, storage pressure, project safety, or another concern
- Any folders you do not want touched

The audit is read-only. No files are moved, renamed, deleted, backed up, or restored without your written approval.

### Delivery Checklist

Before sending the audit PDF, confirm it includes:

- Customer name, job id, audit date, and drive/folder reviewed
- Scope reviewed and any excluded areas
- Duplicate groups or duplicate-clutter summary
- Broken path or risky-area notes when found
- Size summary and highest-clutter areas
- Protected MPC areas such as Projects, Expansions, plugin presets, previews, and _[ProjectData] folders
- Recommended actions
- Clear statement that cleanup requires explicit approval before any deletion
- Guided cleanup add-on note: `$199` after audit approval
